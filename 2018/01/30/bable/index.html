Part 1:

  1. Launch aeris app first;
  2. click ‘create an account’ button;
  3. then click ‘+86’ area, select HK code and save it;
  4. enter an phone number and send a pincode;
  5. enter pincode, then enter email and password and click ‘signup’ button;
  6. go back to signin;

Part 2:

  1. after signin, you should in summary view;
  2. click the top-left button, you will open left menu;
  3. then click ‘device’ button go to device management view;
  4. click ‘+’ button, and select your device’s type;
  5. switch device to listening mode; if it is aair and aura: Please press Smart Mode button for 10 seconds to set your aair in listening mode util screen show ‘I’m listening’; if it is aura: Please press the left button and the power button at the same time for 10 seconds to set your aura in listening mode util the power button is blinking blue;
  6. go to system setting, then connect your phone to device’s wifi: aeris-XXXX;
  7. then go back aeris app to click continue;
  8. enter device name, select an icon, and enter the wifi name and password which you want to connect device to;
  9. click ‘connect’ button and you will go to an connecting view;
  10. waiting until the device connected wifi and jump to the setup success page;
  11. click ‘go to control page’;
  12. now your device is available to control.
